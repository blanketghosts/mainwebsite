title: From Brașov
meta: 2026 · Free verse
---

If I could consume all the darkness
In our cosmos
So that you could be
Surrounded by only the heavens

The 1s, not the 0s
What exists
And nothing of what does not

So that I could dance with you
Like gods without responsibility

Swirling in our stars
Until we become dust upon dust

And forever is just another day in the office
Then I would do just that

But you know I cannot

So instead
I will become poor to make you rich
I will starve to fill your appetite
I will take my vows of solitude
So you can take any man for yourself

And I will sacrifice every convenience
This modern world has to offer
To grant you abundance
From the 10 of Pentacles

Because what good is my gold
If I cannot have you
With our morning coffee
And all the simple things
Only the broken can appreciate

Is this not fair to myself?
Of course it’s not

And that is exactly why it feels like love

Now I leave my heart to you
On the steps of Bran Castle
With the salt of my tears
Preserving what little I have left
To give
