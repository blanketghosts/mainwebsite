---
title: After the Phone Call
meta: 2023 · Free verse
---

I went through the motions
of the rest of the evening:
rinsed the mug,
checked the lock,
sat in the chair that faces the window
and watched a car park slowly across the street.

It took three tries.
The driver got out and looked back at the car
as if to say: close enough.

I thought: yes.
I thought: exactly.
