---
title: The Weight of Small Things
meta: 2024 · Free verse
---

I've been thinking about the spoon
that's been in this drawer for eleven years,
the one that came from nowhere anyone remembers,
with a slight bend at the neck
and a finish gone dull from the machine.

No one claims it.
No one throws it out.

It participates in breakfasts, soups, the occasional
late-night inventory of what we're doing here —
mute, serviceable, present
the way certain people are present
whom we do not appreciate until they leave.
