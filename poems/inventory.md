meta: 2023 · Prose poem

What I have: one good coat, one lamp that buzzes, a jar of beans I keep meaning to cook, a list of names I've carried since the summer I turned twenty-three and didn't know how to say goodbye, a system for the mail that no longer works, a slightly better system for the mail, the word for a smell that arrives before the rain in a language I'm forgetting, three songs that belong to specific years the way a key belongs to a lock, a tendency to underestimate distances, several rooms I can walk through in my sleep.

What I am owed: unclear. What I owe: more than I thought.
