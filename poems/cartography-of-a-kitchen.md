---
title: Cartography of a Kitchen
meta: 2022 · Free verse
---

There are two territories in this kitchen —
the space near the stove, which is mine,
and the rest, which is negotiated.

A peninsula of cups.
A disputed drawer of things
that might be useful,
kept just in case.

We have made peace with the window
which belongs to the light.

Every morning the light moves
and we adjust,
and this seems,
on good days,
like a kind of practice.
