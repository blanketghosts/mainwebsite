title: Grid Studies I–IV
year: 2023
desc: Four black-and-white prints exploring structure and its failure.
---

Four prints made during a period of methodical attention. Each begins with a grid — twelve columns, nine rows — and proceeds by removal. Something is taken from each square: line weight, color, pressure, ink. By the fourth print, the grid is present only as absence, a set of coordinates for something no longer there.

The question the series asks is whether structure survives the removal of its parts. The answer the prints give is not a clear one.
