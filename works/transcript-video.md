Seven minutes of found speech — fragments of interviews, announcements, instructions — played against subtitles that do not match. The subtitles arrive four seconds late, or translate into a language adjacent to the one being spoken, or describe sounds that are not present.

The piece is about the gap between what is said and what is understood. It is also about waiting. Several people who have watched it described feeling that they are about to understand something that keeps not arriving. This is the intended effect.
