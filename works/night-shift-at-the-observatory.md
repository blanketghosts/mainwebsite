title: Night Shift at the Observatory
year: 2023
desc: Two astronomers argue about whether they are discovering or inventing.
---

"You're inventing it," said Marisol, her eye still pressed to the eyepiece. "The whole enterprise. Naming, measuring, mapping. All of it."

"I'm describing it," said Fenn.

"Same thing." She straightened up and made a note. "We point at something and say: there. And by saying there we put it somewhere. Before us it was just —" She gestured at the dome above them, the gap where the roof had slid open to let in the dark.

Fenn considered this. Outside, the mountain was very quiet.

"That seems like a lot of responsibility," he said finally.

"Yes," said Marisol. "It is."
