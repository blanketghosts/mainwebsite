Welcome. This is a space for poetry, music, and other creative work — gathered in one place, without algorithm or advertisement.

I write poems mostly about ordinary things: kitchens, weather, phone calls, the small ceremonies people perform when they think no one is watching. I make music that tends toward the quiet end. I write occasional essays and short fiction.

Everything here was made slowly and with care.
